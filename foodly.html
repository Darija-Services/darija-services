Foodly - Food Recognition and Recommendation App

Introduction

Welcome to Foodly, your go-to app for food recognition and personalized recommendations. Foodly uses advanced image recognition technology to identify foods and offers tailored suggestions based on your preferences.

Features

  • Food Recognition: Upload pictures of your meals, and Foodly will identify the foods present in the images.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Receive customized food recommendations based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, and past choices.
  • Explore New Cuisines: Discover new and exciting dishes from various cuisines around the world.
  • Recipe Suggestions: Get cooking inspiration with recipes related to the recognized foods.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Foodly collects and processes images for food recognition purposes. However, we prioritize user privacy, and we do not store any personally identifiable information. Your data is processed locally on your device, ensuring your privacy is protected.

User Preferences

To enhance your experience, Foodly may store anonymized user preferences on a secure server. This data is used solely to improve the accuracy of food recommendations.

Security

We take security seriously. Any data processed by Foodly is encrypted and stored securely. Rest assured that your information is handled with the utmost care.

Getting Started

  1. Install the App: Download Foodly from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Sign Up or Log In: Create an account or log in to start using the app.
  3. Capture and Explore: Take pictures of your meals, explore recognized foods, and receive personalized recommendations.
